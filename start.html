Where Curiosity Meets Discovery

The Science Club of NIFTEM-T is a student-led community dedicated to exploring the wonders of science through interactive events, workshops, and collaborative projects.

Learn More

Our Mission

The Science Club of NIFTEM-T is a student-led platform that encourages students to explore science in a fun, practical, and meaningful way. Our main aim is to promote scientific thinking and curiosity among students by conducting interactive and educational events.

Interactive Events

We organize quizzes, poster presentations, and model-making competitions to foster a spirit of healthy competition and creativity.

Hands-On Workshops

Our workshops provide practical, hands-on learning experiences, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge.

Expert Guest Lectures

We invite experts from scientific and research backgrounds to inspire and inform students about the latest advancements.

Celebrating Science

We commemorate days of scientific importance like National Science Day and World Food Day with themed events and campaigns.
